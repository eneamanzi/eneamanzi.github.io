This project treats the professional resume not just as a document, but as a piece of software managed through technical tools.

### Why it's a project:
* **LaTeX Source:** Modular structure to generate different versions of the CV (IT/EN) from a single source of truth.
* **Version Control:** Full history of changes managed via Git, ensuring consistency and easy updates.
* **CI/CD Ready:** Optimized for automated building processes and professional output.
* **ATS-Friendly:** Designed with a focus on logical structure and readability for modern recruitment systems.
